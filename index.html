Your virtual Azure cloud architect, out-of-the-box.

Azure is hugely powerful, but also hugely complex. Fathym automates CI/CD, hosting and identity out-of-the-box for deploying and hosting applications in Azure. Our no-code workflows enable junior resources and non developers to test, release and manage apps in your Azure cloud. Fathym provisions Azure services such as Cosmos DB, Blob Storage, App Service, AD B2C, API Gateway and Content Delivery Network out-of-the-box. Take advantage of our simplified user experience while deploying to your own Azure tenant, with full access to your code, infrastructure and additional Azure services. Build a future-proof tech stack from the get-go without the complexity.

Cloud costs you can rely on.

Cloud cost management is a challenge, with 94% of organisations reporting issues with cloud waste. Fathym addresses this by automating and deploying best practice, cost effective infrastructure in your Azure tenant. Fathym's virtual cloud architect charges a small percentage of your Azure cloud consumption costs, ensuring reliable and affordable cloud costs each month.
